Meet The Great Will

I’m William, a digital creator and aspiring voice actor living with SMA Type 2. My condition limits physical movement, but it doesn’t limit creativity. I’m bilingual in English and Spanish, a passionate gamer, and a character actor with a vivid imagination.

Voice acting gives me the power to express what everyday life can sometimes suppress. It’s a place where I can be silly, bold, serious—or all three. Whether I’m a cowboy outlaw or a cartoon cat, I pour my whole self into every role.
